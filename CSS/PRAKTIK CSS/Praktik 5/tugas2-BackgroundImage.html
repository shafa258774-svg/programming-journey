SPIDERMAN

Spider-Man is one of the coolest superheroes ever. His real name is Peter Parker, and he’s just a regular high school kid who gets bitten by a radioactive spider and suddenly gains amazing powers. He can climb walls, swing between buildings using webs he invented, and sense danger with his “spider-sense.” Even though he has super strength and crazy fast reflexes, he still has to deal with homework, money problems, and everyday life in New York City. Created by Stan Lee and Steve Ditko, Spider-Man first appeared in Amazing Fantasy in 1962. What makes him really inspiring is that he always tries to do the right thing because he believes that with great power comes great responsibility.
